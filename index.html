About the Programme

What is the Advance Course?

The Art of Living Advance Course (also known as Part 2) is a powerful residential programme that deepens your meditation practice through ancient Vedic techniques, pranayama, and silence. Guided by trained teachers in a sacred environment, you will experience profound stillness and inner clarity.

This edition is especially blessed — held at the legendary Ved Niketan Ashram in Rishikesh, on the banks of the holy Ganga, surrounded by the Himalayas.

  • 🌬️ Advanced pranayama & Sudarshan Kriya
  • 🧘 Deep meditation practices
  • 🌿 Sacred silence (Maun)
  • 📖 Vedic wisdom & knowledge sessions
  • 🌅 Morning & evening Ganga Aarti

Extended sessions of advanced breathing techniques including Sudarshan Kriya — the cornerstone of the Art of Living practice.

📋 Prerequisites

The Advance Course is open to those who have completed the Art of Living Part 1 (Happiness Programme). If you haven't done Part 1 yet, please complete it before registering.

Location

Ved Niketan Ashram, Rishikesh

Nestled on the banks of the sacred Ganga in Rishikesh's Swargashram area, Ved Niketan Ashram is one of the most revered and serene ashrams in the yoga capital of the world. Founded in 1948, it offers an authentic ashram experience with simple, clean accommodation and a deeply spiritual atmosphere.

📍 Address Swargashram, Rishikesh, Uttarakhand 249304
🚗 From Haridwar ~30 km, approximately 45–60 minutes by road
✈️ Nearest Airport Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun (~25 km)
🚂 Nearest Railway Haridwar Junction (~25 km)
Note: Rishikesh's ashram area is vehicle-free beyond a point. We will park and walk across Laxman Jhula or Ram Jhula to reach the ashram — a beautiful first step of the inner journey.

Preparation

What to Bring

👕 Clothing

  • Loose, comfortable kurtas or salwar suits (white or light colours preferred)
  • Warm layer / shawl (Rishikesh can be cool in September evenings)
  • Comfortable walking sandals
  • Flip-flops for ashram use
  • Light rain jacket (September can bring showers)

🎒 Essentials

  • Government ID (Aadhaar / Passport)
  • Course confirmation / registration receipt
  • Personal medications
  • Yoga Mat
  • Water bottle (stainless steel preferred)
  • Small torch / flashlight

🚫 Leave Behind

  • Laptop (limited or no use)
  • Excess valuables and jewellery
  • Negative thoughts 😊
